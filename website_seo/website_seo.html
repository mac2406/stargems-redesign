Frequently Asked Questions:

Will I own my website after it is developed?

Yes. You will be the full owner of your website, including the domain, hosting access, and website content. Stargems builds and manages the website on your behalf while ensuring you retain complete control.

Do you help with website content and design?

Yes, Stargems can assist with website layout, structure, and content guidance. If you already have content, images, or branding assets, we can incorporate and optimize them to ensure a cohesive and professional look.

Can you redesign or improve my existing website?

Yes, we can redesign or update existing websites to improve performance, user experience, design, or functionality while maintaining your brand identity.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the scope and requirements of the project. Once we review what needs to be done on your website, usually it takes 4-6 weeks once we receive all details. Stargems will share the estimated time required and keep you informed throughout the process.

Can my website integrate with Google Analytics and other tools??

Yes. Stargems can integrate your website with analytics, tracking, and marketing tools to help monitor performance and user behavior.

What is SEO and why is it important?

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the process of improving your website's visibility in search engine results. It helps attract organic traffic, build credibility, and generate long-term growth without relying solely on paid ads.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. While some improvements can be seen within 4 to 5 months, however significant ranking and traffic growth typically takes several more months depending on the competition.

Can you optimize an existing website for SEO?

Yes. Stargems can audit and optimize existing websites to improve structure, content, and performance for better search visibility.
