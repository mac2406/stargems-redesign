The Signature Design

The Signature solitaire ring in yellow gold with an oval cut diamond and a hidden halo.

The Signature Hidden Halo marries our timeless signature setting with the subtle scintillation of The Hidden Halo. The Signature Hidden Halo sits low on the finger, beautifully drawing in the light.

  • Band width: 1.7mm
  • Band depth: 1.6mm
  • Setting height: 4.8mm
  • Gold/Platinum metal weight: 2.95g (size 6)
  • Pavé carat weight: 0.08ctw
  • Shown with center stone: 1.5ct
